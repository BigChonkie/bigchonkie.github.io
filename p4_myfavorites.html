Fav#1 - Book


Percy Jackson Series

This series take place in a world where Greek mythology is real. It takes Greek myths and brings them into modern times. The main character of the book is Percy Jackson, a teenager who finds out that he is the son of Posiedon, the god of the sea. Throughout the series he learns how use his demigod powers, fighting many mythical monsters and creatures along the way. His main goal is to stop the mad titan Kronos from reaching and destroying Mt Olympus.


Fav#2- Book

Hunger Games

The Hunger Games is about a future society that takes place in North America. The country of Panem now rules the continent, and it contains 12 differnt districts, each tasked with managing a specific resource, such as mining, farming, fishing, etc. Each year,the Capital hosts a tournament called the Hunger Games, where 2 people from each district get selected are forced to compete in a fight to the death in a wild arena. Katniss Everdeen, the main protaganist, gets selected and has to fight in the bloodbath.


Fav#3-Movie

Avatar the Way of Water

This movie is a sequel to Avatar. It takes place on a far away moon called Pandora that is full of complex life, including these tall, blue, human like aliens known as the Navi. The movie contains humans who can actually turn into one of these aliens through the avatar system, like the main character Jake Sully. The movie is about how the Navi are trying to defend their home from the humans, who are ravaging every resource they can find.


Fav#4-Movie

Avengers Infinity War

This movie is part of the Avengers series. The Avengers are a team of superheros who protect Earth, but here they are tasked with protecting the universe. A mad titan, known as Thanos, is trying to collect these powerul stones that would allow him to get rid of half the life in the universe. The avengers have to stop him before he does.
